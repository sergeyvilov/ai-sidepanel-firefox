This extension automatically sends pre-defined prompts with the highlighted text to an AI sidebar. Configure up to 5 prompts that appear in the right-click menu or can be invoked through special shortcuts. In your prompt, %s will be automatically substituted with the selected text, %c will be substituted with the snippet including N words before and N words after the selected text, %title will be substituted with the title of the current page, %url will be substituted with the url of the current page. Shortcuts: Click the shortcut field and press your desired key combination. ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini pages with the sidebar: 1. If the sidebar is closed, the prompt is auto-typed into the page input. 2. If the sidebar is opened, the prompt is sent to the sidebar.
