The AI Sidebar extension lets you configure up to 10 prompts that appear in the right-click menu or can be triggered via keyboard shortcuts.

Placeholders in prompt text:

  • %s — selected text
  • %c — selected text with N surrounding words (set via Context size)
  • %title — page title
  • %url — page URL

Shortcuts: Click the shortcut field and press your desired key combination.

On AI provider pages (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Mistral):

  1. Sidebar closed → prompt is typed directly into the page input
  2. Sidebar open → prompt is sent to the sidebar
